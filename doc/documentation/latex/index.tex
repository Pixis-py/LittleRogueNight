\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Little\+Rogue\+Night is a game created for school purpose in {\bfseries{L2 Informatique at Le Mans}}, it is a roguelike based on Little\+Nightmares.
\item Each level features a new configuration of rooms and corridors, making each game unique. The aim is to collect treasures, avoid or fight monsters, foil traps and escape quickly.
\item In this game, you walk, run, jump and fight to escape from a dark place. Beware of monsters, they can be pretty unprevisible...
\end{DoxyItemize}\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md3}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Features}{Features}}\label{index_autotoc_md3}

\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Procedurally Generated Maze\+: Each level offers a new layout of rooms and corridors.
\item Treasures and Riddles\+: Explore and recover hidden treasures while solving riddles.
\item Monsters and Traps\+: Battle a variety of monsters and avoid deadly traps.
\item Dynamic Bosses\+: Powerful bosses chase the hero through the labyrinth at the end of every stage.
\item Store System\+: Buy equipment and weapons to strengthen your character.
\item Secret Zones\+: Discover hidden places brimming with riches and rare items.
\item Reward System\+: Be rewarded according to your solving time, performance and discovers of secret objects/places.
\item Save System \+: Save your current game and start and stop when you want.
